Abstract

Abstract

We form very rapid personality impressions about speakers on hearing a single word. Manon Berriche, Sacha Altay

Journal: Palgrave Communications    Published: 2020-04-28

Abstract

Social media like Facebook are harshly criticized for the propagation of health misinformation.
